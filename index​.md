# OmniFeeds Privacy Policy

OmniFeeds is a local-first app for saving and organizing links, videos, products, notes, and related metadata.

## Data Collection
OmniFeeds does not require a user account and does not collect personal data for tracking or advertising.

## Local Storage
Saved items, thumbnails, metadata, collections, tags, and related organization data are primarily stored locally on the user’s device.

## Shared Content
When you share content into OmniFeeds, the app processes the shared URL or text in order to save and organize it inside the app.

## Metadata and Network Requests
OmniFeeds may request publicly available metadata from shared links, such as titles, descriptions, and thumbnails, in order to improve saved items. These requests are used only to enrich content inside the app.

## Purchases
OmniFeeds offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited saved items. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit.

## Third-Party Services
OmniFeeds does not use third-party advertising SDKs or analytics trackers.

OmniFeeds Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

OmniFeeds is a local-first app for saving, organizing, and finding links, videos, products, notes, and related metadata. This Privacy Policy explains how OmniFeeds handles information.

Summary

OmniFeeds is designed to keep your saved content on your device. The app does not require an account and is not built around advertising or tracking.

Information We Collect

OmniFeeds does not collect personal data for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

The app may process information that you intentionally save into OmniFeeds, including:

  • links and URLs
  • titles, notes, and metadata
  • thumbnails and preview images
  • collections, tags, and organizational labels

This information is primarily stored locally on your device.

Shared Content

When you use the iOS share sheet to send content into OmniFeeds, the app processes the shared URL or text so it can be saved and organized inside the app.

Metadata and Network Requests

To improve saved items, OmniFeeds may request publicly available metadata from links you save. This can include:

  • page titles
  • descriptions
  • thumbnails
  • other publicly available preview information

These requests are only used to enrich content inside the app and are not used for advertising or profiling.

Local Storage

Saved items, metadata, thumbnails, collections, tags, and related organization data are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly export them.

Backups and Export

If you use export or backup features, OmniFeeds creates files that you choose where to store or share. These exported files are under your control.

Purchases

OmniFeeds offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited saved items. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. OmniFeeds does not store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

OmniFeeds does not use third-party advertising SDKs or analytics trackers for user profiling.

Apple may process certain data as part of App Store purchases, device services, or platform infrastructure under Apple’s own policies.

Children’s Privacy

OmniFeeds is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published at this page.
